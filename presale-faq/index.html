Dentacoin Presale FAQ

Make sure you find your answer here. If not, please email us at admin@dentacoin.com so that we don’t miss anything important for you to know!

Start Time: 1st of July 2017, 08:00 a.m. GMT. Check your local time
Exchange Rate: 1 ETH = 8 000 000 DCN
Smart Contract Address: 0x08d32b0da63e2C3bcF8019c9c5d849d7a9d791e6
Recommended Gas Limit: 200 000

ATTN: Never try to buy DCN by sending ETH from any exchange platform. Instead, use the options given above.


+ How can I buy Dentacoin?

During our Exclusive Presale you could buy Dentacoin (DCN) with Ether (ETH) in the following recommended ways:
1) On our website: www.dentacoin.com/presale/ using MetaMask (available for Google Chrome).  Detailed instructions
2) On www.myetherwallet.com by purchasing DCN through ETH from our contract: 0x08d32b0da63e2C3bcF8019c9c5d849d7a9d791e6.  Detailed instructions

If you still don’t have a wallet, make sure you have gone through these steps first:  How to create a wallet?

ATTN: Never try to buy DCN by sending ETH from any exchange platform. Instead, use the options given above.

+ Can I buy DCN through a FIAT currency (e.g. USD, EUR)?

During our Exclusive Presale you could buy Dentacoin through ETH. If you don’t have ETH, you can buy it on an exchange platform (like kraken.com, coinbase.com, bittrex.com, etc.) against your preferred FIAT currency.

ATTN: Keep in mind that the verification process on these platforms recently takes longer than usual (one to two weeks) so you might want to hurry up if you are new to this.

+ What is the fastest way to buy ETH with a credit card?

We recommend you to try with BitPanda: https://www.bitpanda.com/.

+ Can I buy DCN on an exchange platform?

Exchange platforms (like kraken.com, coinbase.com, bittrex.com, etc.) do not support most ERC20 tokens, including Dentacoin. However, you can buy ETH there and then transfer the assets bought to MyEtherWallet.com, MetaMask, MIST, etc.

+ When can I buy Dentacoin?

The Token Presale starts at 8:00 am GMT on July 1st 2017.

+ Can I assure somehow my place at the Presale?

No. You can only make sure you will get reminded by claiming your interest in the Presale here: www.dentacoin.com/presale. The faster you are, the better.

+ What would be the exchange rate DCN/ETH?

During the Presale, the exchange rate DCN/ETH will be 8 000 000 DCN/ETH. In other words: 1 ETH = 8 000 000 DCN.

+ Can I store my ETH and tokens on an exchange platform?

Technically, you could. But we strongly advice you to not do so. Storing any value on an exchange in the long term means that you don't have control over your private key but the exchange does.

+ Do you have a cap for the Presale?

The DCN amount which will be made available during the Presale is 80 000 000 000 Dentacoins (1% of the total Dentacoin supply), meaning that the Contribution Cap will be 10 000 ETH.

+ Is there a minimum or a maximum amount of DCN that I must buy during the Presale?

The minimum purchase requirement is 10 DCN. There is no maximum amount (unless it exceeds the Presale cap) - you can buy just as much as you would like to support our mission: to improve dental care quality worldwide.

+ How much Gas should I use to make sure my ETH transaction goes through?

We recommend you to set the Gas limit to 200 000.

+ Which wallet should I use to contribute?

You can use any wallet where you hold your private key. However, it is preferable to use a wallet which makes it possible for you to add custom tokens and later – to make transfers with them (e.g. MyEtherWallet).

+ When will I see my DCN tokens?

You will receive your Dentacoin immediately. If you don’t see your DCNs, please make sure you use a wallet which could display tokens (e.g. MyEtherWallet). Don’t worry, all wallets can store DCN but not all can display them for now. You can confirm your DCN balance by visiting:
https://etherscan.io/token/0x08d32b0da63e2C3bcF8019c9c5d849d7a9d791e6?a=“enter-your-eth-address-without-quotes”

+ How can I find my transaction ID?

The transaction ID is mostly called TxHash.
To see your Transaction ID:
1) Go to ETH & ERC20:  https://etherscan.io
2) Type in the address you sent ETH from and you’ll be able to see your Transaction ID/TxHash.

+ How are you going to use the funds from the Presale?

The funds collected will be allocated for:
1) Creating a team of front- and back-end developers for the Trusted Review Platform Testnet Launch.
2) Programming and launching the Trusted Review Platform (testnet).
3) Programming and launching supporting tools: aftercare mobile app, educational website.



ATTN: Please, do NOT send funds to any direct addresses posted in Slack, Facebook, Reddit or any other social media to avoid fraud. Trust only the information on our official website.
