Dentacoin Roadmap

01

Dentacoin Integrated Platform

The next major update of Dentacoin will consist of integrating all developed apps in one place. Dentacoin Platform will seamlessly connect all main market participants within the dental industry – dentists, patients, suppliers, labs, and related service companies. It will be the online space where those parties are able to interact with each other easy and fast, to exchange valuable feedback and be rewarded for their contribution. The Blockchain base of the platform allows a sophisticated incentive system, manipulation protection, seamless payment transactions, proven identities and transparency.

02

Decentralized Health Database

A decentralized dental health database stores patient’s data in a secure and reliable way. It is fully controlled by patients - only they decide what to store in the database and who has access to it. This peerless security degree and control over one’s own data is ensured by the Blockchain. For dentists, that means easier and more precise prophylaxis plans and highly individualized treatment plans based on a holistic view of the patient health.

03

DENTACOIN ASSURANCE

Dentacoin Assurance is a revolutionary dental insurance-like program, which encourages paying for prevention and not for treatment. It is executed through a smart contract on the Blockchain, directly between patients and dentists. Through low monthly premium in Dentacoin paid to their dentists, patients are assured to receive lifelong, prevention-oriented dental care. The model brings the financial interests of patients and dentists into complete alignment and establishes strong, long-lasting bonds.

04

THE LARGEST DENTAL NETWORK

Blockchain-based tools and a common industry-specific currency, dental professionals, patients, suppliers, dental laboratories, service agencies are seamlessly connected, able to efficiently cooperate with each other and to generate personal and mutual benefits. Within this newly-created network, network effects are triggered meaning that each new participant increases the value of the network for all existing participants. The earliest participants generate the most benefits.
