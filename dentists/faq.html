Frequently Asked Questions

01 What is Dentacoin Platform?
The Dentacoin Platform is an emerging software platform that provides different solutions in the form of tools and applications, which benefit the industry and its participants - currently, we are mostly focused on both the practitioners (dentists/clinics) and the patients as a main target groups, providing specific incentives for both groups. The ground level of the Dentacoin platform is DCN - an ERC20 token, part of the Ethereum Network and built on top of the Ethereum Blockchain. DCN is used as a reward system, and it is the main engine of the Dentacoin Foundation.
02 What is DCN?
DCN (Dentacoin) is part of the Dentacoin Platform infrastructure. DCN is developed on the Ethereum network, as ETC-20 token. Here is a link that provides you with all the information you may need in order to verify the legitimacy of the smart contract:
https://etherscan.io/token/0x08d32b0da63e2C3bcF8019c9c5d849d7a9d791e6

05 Dentacoin Tools?
Dentacoin Platform provides its users with different tools some of them are Trusted review platform - The first Blockchain-based platform for trusted dental treatment reviews to allow patients to raise their voice and have an impact on the global dental industry:

  • Trusted Reviews - The first Blockchain-based platform for trusted dental treatment reviews to allow patients to raise their voice and have an impact on the global dental industry.
  • Dentacare - An interactive aftercare app to assist patients in steps to be followed post-treatment, aiming at developing long-lasting oral hygiene habits in both children and adults.
  • Dentavox - A WebApp for collective customer wisdom, which serves as an up-to-date market research data through questionnaires and rewards. The dentist can have access to this valuable information and apply this "wisdom" to improve.
