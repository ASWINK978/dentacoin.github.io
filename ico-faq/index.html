Where can I store Dentacoin (DCN)?

We strongly advice you to not store your Dentacoin (DCN) on exchange platforms. Storing any value on an exchange in the long term means that you don't have control over your private key but the exchange does.
For storing Dentacoin we recommend you to use an Ethereum wallet clients where you hold your private key (e.g. MyEtherWallet, MetaMask, imToken, Coinomi). For a next level of security, you can consider a hardware wallet such as TREZOR or Ledger Nano S.

How can I find my transaction ID?

The transaction ID is mostly called TxHash.
To see your Transaction ID:
1) Go to ETH & ERC20: https://etherscan.io
2) Type in the address you sent ETH from and you’ll be able to see your Transaction ID / TxHash.
